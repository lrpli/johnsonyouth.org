Our Mission

What drives everything we do for young people and families in our community.

Johnson Youth Organization Inc exists to equip youth with mentorship, educational support, and leadership opportunities so they can thrive in school, family, and community life.

We believe every young person deserves consistent encouragement, safe spaces, and access to adults who believe in their potential.

visibility Looking Forward

Our Vision

We envision a future where young people in Blythe and neighboring communities are empowered to lead with confidence, compassion, and purpose -- creating healthier families and stronger neighborhoods for generations to come.
